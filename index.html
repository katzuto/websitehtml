What is Metaclerk?
Non-fungible tokens are becoming more and more populae. This space is filled with investing opportunities. However with so many projects already launched and new projects are launching every day. It has become increasingly difficult to keep track of all the projects you are invested in and all the NFT drops and tokens you could be receiving if you were up to date on each project. This is why we created Metaclerk. Our Goal is to drastically decrease time spent on repetitive tasks like checking announcement channels in discord, clicking through telegram pins and scrolling through twitter to catch the next big thing. Metaclerk ist the personal assistant everyone has been waiting for.
How does Metaclerk work?
Our first tool is going to give you an overview of all current and future drops you are eligible to receive. Not only will you be able to see which projects are in need of immediate attention and action but you will also be able to claim your drops directly on our site. This is only one of many tools we plan to implement. Maximum efficency is the name of the game.
